eFACiLiTY Stores and Inventory Management System facilitates the storekeepers to manage their entire operations seamlessly. The system enables the store management teams in manufacturing, distribution, retail facilities, etc., to efficiently manage inventory, track items, generate purchase orders, accurately track inventory costs & inventory levels in real-time.

It is an easy-to-use software that can be implemented to manage all types of inventory like spare parts, consumables, tools, fuel, printing & stationery, kitchen inventory, waste to be sent for recycling, etc. eFACiLiTY Stores and Inventory Management software includes features like inventory level tracking, re-stocking orders, low stock threshold alerts, and extensive reporting capability making it comprehensive and fully automated.
